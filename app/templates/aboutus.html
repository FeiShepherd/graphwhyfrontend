GraphWhy.org

At GraphWhy.org, we are ‘building statistical understanding’. Our main goal here at GraphWhy is to build an archive and database of people’s beliefs so that our society can have an extensive resource to understand why our world is the way it is.

For example, a student is writing a paper on why our national debt has grown to the size that it is now. In addition to some economic and political theory, the student wants to explore how U.S. citizens feel about the national debt. Using GraphWhy the student has a set of tools perfect for exploring how citizens feel about the national debt.
